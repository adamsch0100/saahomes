# Competitor Content Watch

## Weekly scan

### Targets
Identify 5–8 local broker team sites ranking for our Tier S city queries (from SERP or competitor-intel.md).

### For each competitor
1. Check homepage /blog /areas for **new or updated pages** since last scan (compare sitemap lastmod, RSS, or cached dates)
2. Note new city/neighborhood landing pages
3. Note new blog posts targeting our keywords
4. Note market report or seller lead magnet launches

### Threat assessment
| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 🔴 High | New page targeting our city + outranking us or fresh content on query we rank #4–10 |
| 🟡 Medium | New content in our market but different query |
| 🟢 Low | Generic national content |

### Response required for 🔴
Within report, include:
- Competitor URL
- Query at risk
- Our defending URL
- Proposed response (expand area page, new blog, internal links) with 7-day timeline

## Output

```
🔭 Competitor Watch — Week of [date]

New threats
1. [competitor] published [URL] targeting [query] — THREAT: [H/M/L]

Recommended responses
1. ...

Update competitor-intel.md with findings
```

## Constraints
- Public pages only, respectful crawl rate
- Do not copy content — analyze and out-depth
