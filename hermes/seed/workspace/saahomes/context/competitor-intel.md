# Competitor Intelligence — Northern Colorado Real Estate

Hermes maintains this file. Update after each `competitor-content-watch` run.

## Who we're beating

Schwartz and Associates wins on:
- **19 dedicated area guide pages** (most solo agents have 0–3)
- **CHFA program cluster** (DPA, Schools to Home, Champions) — rare depth locally
- **Prerender + schema** stack on saahomes.com
- **Local video** (YouTube @SAAHomes) + testimonials
- **Named agents** (Adam & Mandi) vs anonymous portal leads

## Competitor categories to monitor

### Tier 1 — Local broker teams (direct SEO rivals)
Monitor weekly for new area content, blog posts, market reports:
- Other Fort Collins / Loveland / Windsor team sites
- Coldwell Banker competing teams in Larimer County
- RE/MAX, Keller Williams, Berkshire teams with strong local SEO

**Watch signals:** new `/areas/` or `/communities/` pages, new blog cadence, updated market reports, GBP post frequency

### Tier 2 — Portals (ranking competitors, not brand rivals)
- Zillow.com
- Realtor.com
- Redfin.com
- Homes.com

**Strategy:** outrank with local expert content + E-E-A-T; convert with "work with a local expert" CTAs. Don't try to out-feature their listing search — funnel to `/properties/` + contact.

### Tier 3 — Aggregators / lead gen
- HomeLight, FastExpert, etc.

**Strategy:** capture same queries with superior local content; emphasize no lead-reselling.

## Content response SLA

When a Tier 1 competitor publishes content targeting our city:
1. **Within 48h:** assess threat (do they outrank us? better depth?)
2. **Within 7 days:** propose response (area page expansion, blog post, internal links)
3. **Log in MEMORY wins** when we regain position
