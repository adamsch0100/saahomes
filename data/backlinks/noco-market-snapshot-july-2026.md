## Key Trends

1. **Inventory improving**: 12-15% more homes on market vs July 2025
2. **Price stabilization**: Appreciation slowing to 4-6% annual rate
3. **Days on market increasing**: Averaging 38 days across NoCo
4. **New construction adding supply**: Timnath, Wellington, Johnstown leading

## Buyer Opportunities

- CHFA down payment grants up to $25,000 for first-time buyers
- VA zero-down loans available for veterans and active duty
- Negotiating power strongest in $500K+ price points
- Interest rates stabilizing, improving affordability

## Seller Strategies

- Competitive pricing essential — overpriced listings sit 60+ days

- First 14 days on market are critical for momentum
