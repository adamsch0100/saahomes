## CHFA Down Payment Assistance

Colorado first-time homebuyers can access **up to $25,000** in down payment help:

- **SmartStep**: 3% grant or 4% second mortgage
- **FirstStep**: Lower rates, 4% DPA
- **Schools to Home**: Teacher program
- **Colorado Champions**: First responder program
