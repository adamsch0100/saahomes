# Weld County Small Towns Real Estate Guide 2026

## Eaton & La Salle — Northern Colorado's Hidden Gems

Beyond Fort Collins, Loveland, and Windsor lie two Weld County towns offering exceptional value for Colorado homebuyers. [SAA Homes](https://saahomes.com) has helped families find homes in these communities for over 20 years.

### Eaton, Colorado
Eaton combines small-town charm with easy access to Greeley. The **Eaton RE-2 School District** is consistently ranked among Colorado's best small-town districts. Median home prices around $475,000 make Eaton significantly more affordable than Fort Collins or Windsor.
